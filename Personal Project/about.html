Dr. Anthony Fisher

General Practioner

Dr Anthony Fisher has been a life-long Transylvania County resident other than traveling with his family as a child while his father was in the Army and attending college. He has a Master of Arts degree in English from Western Carolina University, a Doctor of Medicine degree from University of North Carolina-Chapel Hill, and completed his residency in Emergency Medicine at Baystate Medical Center in Massachusetts. He has served as the Medical Director for Transylvania County Emergency Medical Services since 1997. He was recently a volunteer/staff physician at the Transylvania County Free Medical Clinic, is currently on the Transylvania County Board of Health, and is the Director for the Transylvania County Wellness Center.
